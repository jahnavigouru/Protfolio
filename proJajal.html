    • Backstory

    MySegmenter is an online application developed by Jajal Medical services that digitalizes complex surgery planning in 3D. The process starts by uploading a CT scan of the patient onto the MySegmenter platform, the data is then imported to the segmentation software which further gets converted into a 3D model for visualization. MySegmenter is Asia's first Virtual Surgical Planning platform for complex surgeries.
    Temporarily the compay was using Mimics, Simpleware softwares for 3D image processing and model generation but they wanted to be future-ready for Automatic Segmentation tools. Hence, 3D Slicer an open source software was being taken as a reference for developing an in-house standalone desktop application called MySegmenter for Windows for 3D visualization and segmentation.

    Features

    3D Slicer

    3D Slicer has four views of the patient's CT scan which are; Axial(from top to down), Sagittal (side to side), Coronal (from front to back), and the 4th one is the 3d aspect of the data. Slice view names of Axial, Sagittal, and Coronal are Red, Green, and Yellow respectively.

    MySegmenter

    The modifications the company wanted in Mysegmenter were to have:

  • A vertical Slice offset slider on the extreme right of each view.
  • A Maximize/minimize button: This helps the user to maximize the selected view occupying the entire view layout and minimizes when clicked again (top right corner of every view).
  • Add Labels representing which orientation each view belongs to(Axial, Sagittal, Coronal).
  • Left(L), Top(T), Right(R), and Bottom(B) labels for every view.
  • And an option to switch to any desired orientation in form of a dropdown for each view.
